Majority Colouring Game

Level

Hello World! On this website you can play a puzzle game which is based on graph theory. Rules are simple: you need to end up with a majority-coloured graph. This means that each vertex, let's call it v, coloured with colour c(v) must have at most half of its adjacent vertices coloured c(v). You can change a vertex color into green by clicking on it. Keep in mind that your computer opponent will color one vertex red after your move. The opponent aims to prevent from majority colouring. Sounds challenging? Hit the play button and have fun!
